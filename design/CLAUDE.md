# Drivers Against Flock — project notes

## Contact email policy
Wherever an `@driversagainstflock.com` / `@driversagainstflock.org` email address is shown
anywhere in the app, also show `driversagainstflock@proton.me` alongside it as an alternate option.
The `driversagainstflock@proton.me` address alone also appears centered (no label) in the bottom
footer of every page template.
